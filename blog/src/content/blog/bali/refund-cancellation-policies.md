## Understanding Cancellation Policies for Bali

Life is unpredictable, and travel plans can change. Understanding the cancellation and refund policies for your Bali bookings helps protect your investment and reduces anxiety. Policies vary significantly between providers, so knowing what to look for before you book is essential.

## Hotel Cancellation Policies

### Booking.com and OTA Platforms
Most Bali hotels on booking platforms offer two rate types:
- **Flexible rate:** Free cancellation up to 24-72 hours before check-in. Slightly higher price (5-15% more).
- **Non-refundable rate:** Cheapest price but no refund if you cancel. Some offer date changes instead.

### Direct Hotel Bookings
When booking directly with a Bali hotel:
- Policies vary widely — always confirm before paying
- Many require a **deposit (30-50%)** at booking with the balance due on arrival
- Cancellation typically free **7-30 days before arrival** depending on the property
- **Peak season bookings** (July-August, Christmas/New Year) often have stricter cancellation terms
- Luxury resorts may require **full prepayment** for holiday periods

### Tips
- Always book flexible rates for trips more than 3 months away
- Switch to non-refundable once you are certain of your dates
- Read the fine print — "free cancellation" sometimes means credit rather than refund
- Direct bookings may offer more flexibility in renegotiating terms

## Activity and Tour Cancellation

### Organized Tours and Day Trips
Most Bali tour operators follow similar policies:
- **Free cancellation:** 24-48 hours before the activity
- **50% refund:** 12-24 hours before
- **No refund:** Less than 12 hours or no-show
- **Weather cancellations:** Usually rescheduled free of charge or full refund

### Booking Platforms (Klook, GetYourGuide, Viator)
- Most activities offer **free cancellation 24 hours before**
- "Instant confirmation" bookings may have different cancellation terms
- Refund processing takes 5-14 business days
- Some activities are marked "non-refundable" — check before booking

### Private Drivers
- Most are flexible and can be cancelled or rescheduled same-day
- Pre-paid bookings through agencies may have 24-hour cancellation policies

## Flight Cancellation

### Airlines
Policies vary by airline and fare class:
- **Budget airlines (AirAsia, Lion Air):** Often non-refundable. May offer credit for future flights minus a change fee.
- **Full-service airlines (Garuda, Singapore Airlines):** Flexible fares allow free changes/cancellation. Economy fares typically charge change fees of USD 50-150.
- **Most airlines** offer free cancellation within 24 hours of booking (check airline terms)

### Travel Insurance Coverage
Good travel insurance covers flight cancellation for covered reasons:
- Illness or injury
- Death of immediate family member
- Natural disasters
- Jury duty or court summons
- Job loss (some policies)

**Not typically covered:** Change of mind, work schedule changes, finding a cheaper flight

## Fast Boat Cancellation (Nusa Penida/Gili Islands)

- Most operators offer **full refund for weather cancellations**
- Cancellation by the traveler: varies, typically 24-48 hours notice for full refund
- **Sea conditions** may cause cancellations during wet season — choose operators with clear policies

## Protecting Your Investment

### Before Booking
- Read cancellation policy before paying — never assume
- Screenshot the terms in case they change
- Choose flexible rates for uncertain plans
- Pay by credit card for additional buyer protection

### Travel Insurance
- Purchase comprehensive travel insurance that covers trip cancellation
- Ensure it covers your specific reasons for potential cancellation
- "Cancel for any reason" (CFAR) add-ons provide the most flexibility but cost 40-60% more

### Documentation
- Keep confirmation emails and booking references
- Save screenshots of cancellation policies
- Document any communication about changes or cancellations
- Keep receipts for any alternative arrangements

Understanding cancellation policies before you book is far easier than negotiating refunds after you cancel. A few minutes of reading fine print can save significant stress and money.
