## Romantic Stays in Delhi

Delhi may not be the first city that comes to mind for romance, but look beyond the chaos and you will find heritage hotels with candlelit courtyards, luxury resorts with private plunge pools, and intimate restaurants that rival the most romantic cities in the world. For couples visiting as part of a larger India trip, or honeymooners passing through the capital, these properties offer the perfect blend of romance, luxury, and cultural immersion.

## Ultra-Luxury Romantic Hotels

### The Lodhi, Lodhi Road
**From ₹25,000 per night**
Delhi's most romantic hotel. Every suite features a private plunge pool, and the property's serene design creates an intimate atmosphere despite being in central Delhi. The spa offers couple's treatment rooms, and Indian Accent restaurant on-site is perfect for a special dinner. Request the Garden Suite for the most private experience.
- **Romance factor:** Private pools, butler service, Indian Accent restaurant
- **Best for:** Honeymooners with a generous budget

### Aman New Delhi, Lodhi Road
**From ₹45,000 per night**
The pinnacle of intimate luxury, Aman's 40 suites each feature private gardens or courtyards. The spa, with its serene treatment rooms and meditation spaces, is among Asia's finest. The poolside lounge offers secluded dining experiences.
- **Romance factor:** Extreme privacy, world-class spa, private dining
- **Best for:** Once-in-a-lifetime romantic splurge

### The Imperial, Janpath
**From ₹18,000 per night**
Old-world glamour at its finest. The Imperial's heritage suites with four-poster beds, art-lined corridors, and the legendary 1911 bar create a uniquely romantic atmosphere. The Atrium is ideal for afternoon tea together.
- **Romance factor:** Heritage charm, art collection, Spice Route restaurant
- **Best for:** Couples who appreciate history and culture

### The Leela Palace, Chanakyapuri
**From ₹20,000 per night**
Lutyens-inspired architecture with grand public spaces and beautifully appointed rooms. The Royal Club Suites offer a premium romantic experience with lounge access, evening cocktails, and canapes.
- **Romance factor:** Grand architecture, ESPA spa, rooftop bar
- **Best for:** Couples seeking classic luxury

## Boutique and Mid-Range Romantic Hotels

### Roseate House, Aerocity
**From ₹10,000 per night**
A design-forward hotel with dramatic interiors, an excellent spa, and a serene infinity pool. The modern aesthetic and intimate scale (less than 200 rooms) make it feel more boutique than business.
- **Romance factor:** Design interiors, spa, intimate atmosphere
- **Best for:** Design-conscious couples

### Haveli Dharampura, Old Delhi
**From ₹8,000 per night**
A beautifully restored 19th-century haveli (mansion) in the heart of Old Delhi. The rooftop terrace overlooking Jama Masjid is one of Delhi's most romantic dinner spots. Each room is uniquely decorated with traditional Mughal-era aesthetics.
- **Romance factor:** Heritage ambiance, rooftop dining, Old Delhi charm
- **Best for:** Couples seeking cultural immersion and unique stays

### The Manor, Friends Colony
**From ₹12,000 per night**
A intimate boutique hotel with just 16 rooms in leafy South Delhi. The personal service, quiet gardens, and acclaimed Indian Accent restaurant (before its move to The Lodhi) heritage make it a refined romantic choice.
- **Romance factor:** Intimate scale, quiet gardens, personalized service
- **Best for:** Couples who prefer boutique over big hotel

## Romantic Experiences for Couples

### Dining
- **Candlelight dinner at Indian Accent** — reserve the corner table (₹4,000-6,000 per person)
- **Rooftop dinner at Haveli Dharampura** — Old Delhi views with Mughal cuisine
- **Cocktails at PCO** — a speakeasy-style bar in Khan Market
- **Sunday brunch at Olive Bar & Kitchen** — Mediterranean cuisine in a romantic white courtyard

### Spa Experiences
- **Couple's treatment at The Oberoi Spa** — traditional Ayurvedic therapies (₹8,000-15,000 per couple)
- **ESPA at The Leela Palace** — luxury treatments with imported products (₹6,000-12,000)
- **Aman Spa** — serene meditation and wellness experience (₹10,000-20,000)

### Activities
- **Sunrise at Humayun's Tomb** — arrive at opening time for a virtually private experience at this stunning monument
- **Sunset at Lodhi Gardens** — a romantic walk among ancient tombs and rose gardens
- **Moonlit walk at India Gate** — the illuminated monument is magical after dark
- **Heritage walk in Old Delhi** — discover hidden havelis and love stories from the Mughal era
- **Cooking class together** — learn to make Mughlai cuisine at various cooking schools (₹3,000-5,000 per couple)

## Tips for Couples

- **Book honeymoon packages** — many luxury hotels offer packages including spa treatments, champagne, and room upgrades
- **Mention your occasion** when booking — hotels often provide complimentary cake, flowers, or room decoration for anniversaries and honeymoons
- **Request a quiet room** — ask for a higher floor away from the elevator
- **Public displays of affection** are generally frowned upon in India — keep physical affection for private spaces
- **Delhi is a gateway** — combine with a trip to Agra (Taj Mahal), Jaipur (Pink City), or Udaipur (City of Lakes) for a full romantic India experience
