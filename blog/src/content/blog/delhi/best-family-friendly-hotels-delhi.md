## Family Hotels in Delhi

Traveling with children requires hotels that go beyond a comfortable bed — you need space, kid-friendly amenities, safe environments, and convenient locations. Delhi's hotel scene caters well to families, with many properties offering interconnecting rooms, children's programs, swimming pools, and family dining options.

## Luxury Family Hotels

### The Oberoi, Dr. Zakir Husain Marg
**From ₹22,000 per night**
The Oberoi's spacious rooms and suites are ideal for families. The large swimming pool, well-maintained gardens, and attentive staff who dote on young guests make it a top family choice. Interconnecting rooms available. Children's dining menu at all restaurants.
- **Family highlights:** Pool, garden area, kids' amenities pack on arrival
- **Location:** Central Delhi near Humayun's Tomb

### Taj Palace, Sardar Patel Marg
**From ₹14,000 per night**
Set in six acres of gardens — a rarity in Delhi — the Taj Palace gives families room to breathe. Children love the expansive lawns, the pool, and the variety of restaurants. The hotel can arrange kids' activities and babysitting.
- **Family highlights:** Large gardens, pool, kids' menu, babysitting service
- **Location:** Diplomatic Enclave, quiet neighborhood

### ITC Maurya, Diplomatic Enclave
**From ₹16,000 per night**
Spacious rooms, excellent in-room dining, and the legendary Bukhara restaurant where kids eat with their hands (everyone does). The concierge can arrange family-oriented heritage tours and activities.
- **Family highlights:** Spacious suites, multiple restaurants, concierge services
- **Location:** Chanakyapuri

### The Leela Palace, Chanakyapuri
**From ₹20,000 per night**
Modern luxury with family-sized suites, a large swimming pool, and a kids' activity program. The hotel's central courtyard is a pleasant space for children to explore.
- **Family highlights:** Kids' program, pool, interconnecting rooms
- **Location:** Near Rashtrapati Bhawan

## Mid-Range Family Hotels

### Radisson Blu Plaza, Mahipalpur
**From ₹6,500 per night**
Reliable international chain quality with family rooms, a swimming pool, and multiple dining options. The location near the airport is convenient for families arriving late or departing early.
- **Family highlights:** Pool, family rooms, airport shuttle
- **Location:** Near IGI Airport

### Holiday Inn New Delhi International Airport, Aerocity
**From ₹6,000 per night**
Families appreciate the consistent quality, kids-stay-free policy, large rooms, and direct metro access to the city. The breakfast buffet is extensive and kid-friendly.
- **Family highlights:** Kids stay free, pool, metro connectivity
- **Location:** Aerocity

### The Claridges, Aurangzeb Road
**From ₹8,000 per night**
A heritage property with gracious service and enough space for families to spread out. The garden area and pool are excellent for children, and the central location makes sightseeing convenient.
- **Family highlights:** Heritage charm, gardens, pool, central location
- **Location:** Near Lodhi Gardens

### Pullman New Delhi Aerocity
**From ₹7,000 per night**
Modern property with family-oriented amenities including a kids' corner, swimming pool, and spacious rooms. The Aerocity location offers easy airport access and metro connectivity.
- **Family highlights:** Kids' corner, pool, family rooms
- **Location:** Aerocity

## Budget Family Hotels

### Bloomrooms @ Janpath
**From ₹3,500 per night**
A smart budget hotel in a fantastic location for families who want to be in the center of things. Rooms are compact but well-designed. The Janpath location puts you within walking distance of Connaught Place and India Gate.
- **Family highlights:** Central location, modern design, cafe
- **Location:** Janpath, near Connaught Place

### The Park New Delhi
**From ₹5,000 per night**
A trendy mid-range hotel with a good pool and central location. The rooms are modern and well-equipped, and the hotel has a lively restaurant scene that works for families during the day.
- **Family highlights:** Pool, central CP location, modern rooms
- **Location:** Connaught Place area

## What to Look for in Family Hotels

### Essential Features
- **Interconnecting rooms** or family suites with separate sleeping areas
- **Swimming pool** — a must-have for keeping kids entertained between sightseeing
- **In-room safe** for passports and valuables
- **24-hour room service** — essential when kids are hungry at odd hours
- **Reliable Wi-Fi** for evening entertainment
- **Good breakfast buffet** with kid-friendly options

### Nice-to-Have Features
- **Babysitting services** for a parents-only dinner
- **Kids' club or activity room**
- **Garden or outdoor space**
- **Laundry service** (kids generate laundry faster than adults)
- **Proximity to medical facilities** for peace of mind

## Tips for Booking

- **Request a high floor** for quieter rooms away from street noise
- **Ask about extra bed/crib policies** — many hotels provide free cribs, but extra beds may incur charges
- **Kids-stay-free policies** are common at international chains but verify age limits
- **Book directly** with the hotel for the best flexibility on room configurations
- **Mention traveling with children** when booking — many hotels arrange welcome amenities for young guests
- **Choose hotels near metro stations** to avoid long cab rides with restless children
